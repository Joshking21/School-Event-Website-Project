ABOUT US

The Green GHost Hackathon began in 2015 as a thrilling convergence of brilliant minds di]riven by a shared passion for technological innovation. The event quickly garnered attention, thanks to its unique format that challenges participants to develop groundbreaking applications and prototypes
From its humble beginnings with just a handful of participants in a local community center, the hackathon has grown into an international event, attracting innovators from around the globe.

OUR MISSION

At the core of our hackathon is a belief in the transformative power of innovation and collaboration. Our mission is to create a dynamic platform where talented individuals can come together to tackle real-world challenges, sparking creativity and fostering the development of solutions that make a lasting impact.
By cultivating an inclusive and inspiring environment, we aim to drive progress and ignite a passion for technology and problem-solving in everyone who joins our hackathon.
